About Us

Welcome to Vshakha Fashion, a premier wholesale garment supplier based in Surat, Gujarat. With years of experience in the textile industry, we specialize in providing high-quality ethnic and western wear for men, women, and children.

Our extensive collection includes traditional kurtas, nightwear, track pants, western tops, chaniya choli sets, and much more. We take pride in offering trendy designs, superior fabric quality, and competitive wholesale pricing.

At Vshakha Fashion, we understand the dynamics of the retail market and strive to provide our clients with products that ensure maximum customer satisfaction and profitability.
